How we Started

The museum was founded by John Smith in 1960. He was a resident of Stratford back when it was a small , unknown town in the province of Ontario. Him and his team built the museum from the ground up in order to bring more awareness to the beauty the town of Stratford had to offer. Over the next 5 years before the opening, he partnered with art museums all around Canada in order to fill his museum with the most beautiful artwork. After 2 decades TSAG began to grow and attract more attention from galleries all around the world and now become what it is today.
